About us

Hi I am Pratishtha Mandal, a Mixed Media artist, from Maharashtra, India.
I have explored various form of artist medium, watercolors and acrlyic being
the strongest medium of painting for me.
My artist journey started back in 2011, been making art since then.

"Pratishtha Art" came into existence in the year 2018.


I make abstract paintings, portraits using acylic/watercolor/charcoal,
Realistic painting and many such.
Since then I have sold over 100+ artwork, and hope to sell more.
